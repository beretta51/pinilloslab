How Smart Activity Cards Organize Your Entire Trip

April 14, 2026

Planning a trip usually means juggling notes, screenshots, and spreadsheets. Trovelo takes a different approach — you just type what you're doing, and the app structures everything for you.

What Are Smart Activity Cards?

Every activity in your itinerary lives on its own card. Whether it's a flight, a hotel check-in, a dinner reservation, or a museum visit, each card captures the details that matter: time, location, cost, and notes.

The magic is in how they're created. Just type naturally — "Dinner at La Piazzetta, 8pm, €45 per person" — and Trovelo automatically categorizes it, extracts the time and cost, and slots it into the right day.

Built for Real Trips

Smart Activity Cards support every type of travel moment:

  • Flights — departure, arrival, airline, terminal
  • Hotels — check-in/out dates, confirmation number
  • Restaurants — time, cuisine, budget
  • Transport — trains, buses, taxis, rentals
  • Expenses — tracked in dual currency automatically

Everything shows up on an interactive map so you can see your day at a glance and optimize your route.
