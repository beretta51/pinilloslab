Getting started

  • Create lists. Make color-coded lists for work, routines, groceries, or anything else, and add tasks with notes and checklists inside them.
  • Set alarms. Give a task an alarm so it pings you at the right moment.
  • Track habits. Check off your daily habits and keep your streaks alive.
  • Keep score. Use the built-in Scoreboard to keep score during game nights.

Free plan & the unlimited upgrade

Dimmly is free for up to 5 lists. A one-time purchase unlocks unlimited lists — no subscription. The purchase is tied to your Apple Account, so one purchase covers your iPhone and Mac. Use Restore Purchases in Settings if the upgrade doesn't show up on a new device.

iCloud sync

There are no accounts and no Dimmly servers. Your tasks and habits stay on your device and sync across your devices privately through your own iCloud account whenever you're signed in to iCloud. There's nothing to configure.

Privacy

Dimmly has no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics. Read the full Dimmly Privacy Policy.
