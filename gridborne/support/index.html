Gridborne Support

A turn-based strategy game played on a 7×7 grid. Quiet rules, deep tactics. Built for short, satisfying matches.

Need help?

Email nocion.exento_8u@icloud.com and I'll get back to you. Gridborne is made by an independent developer, Eduardo Pinillos — a real person reads every message.

Getting started

  • Learn as you play. The rules are quiet on purpose — place your units on the 7×7 board and the game teaches you the rest as you go.
  • Short matches. Games are built to be short and satisfying, so a match fits in a coffee break.
  • Fully offline. Gridborne runs entirely on your device — no internet connection needed, ever.

Privacy

Gridborne collects no data and has no tracking — the game runs entirely on your device, offline. Read the full Gridborne Privacy Policy.
