Overview

Trovelo is a travel planning app designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

Data Storage

All information you enter in Trovelo — including trip names, destinations, dates, activities, expenses, notes, and any other content — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's native on-device storage. Your data never leaves your iPhone.

No Data Collection

Trovelo does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any identifying information
  • We do not use analytics or crash reporting tools
  • We do not use advertising or third-party tracking
  • We do not have servers or databases that store your information
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Third-Party Services

Trovelo uses the following Apple-provided services, which are governed by Apple's own privacy policy:

  • MapKit — used to search for places when planning trips. Searches are processed by Apple Maps.
  • Open-Meteo — used to display weather forecasts. Only your trip's city name is sent to retrieve weather data. Open-Meteo is a free, open-source weather API that does not store personal data.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all Trovelo data at any time by deleting the app from your iPhone. No data remains on any server because none was ever sent.

Children's Privacy

Trovelo does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Overview

Percha is designed with privacy in mind. The app works entirely on your device — no account is required and no personal data is sent to external servers.

Data We Collect

Percha does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter (clothing items, photos, outfits) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and is never uploaded anywhere.

Permissions

Camera

Used only to photograph your clothing items. Photos are saved locally on your device and are never transmitted.

Photo Library

Used only to import existing photos of your clothing. Access is granted only when you choose to import a photo.

Location (When In Use)

Used only to fetch local weather data to power outfit suggestions. Your location is sent directly to Apple's WeatherKit service and is never stored or shared by Percha.

Third-Party Services

Percha uses Apple WeatherKit to provide weather-based outfit suggestions. This service is governed by Apple's own privacy policy. No other third-party services are used.

Data Deletion

All your data is stored on your device. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app.
