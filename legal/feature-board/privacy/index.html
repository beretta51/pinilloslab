Privacy Policy — feature-board

Last updated: July 22, 2026

What this app does

feature-board displays a feature-request board inside a Reddit post. Requests are created from public posts in the subreddit that carry the "Feature Request" flair.

What data it stores

The app stores, within Reddit's own infrastructure (Devvit Redis):

  • A copy of public post content: title, body, author username, creation date, and a link to the original post.
  • Vote records: the Reddit user ID of each user who votes on a request, used solely to enforce one vote per user.

What it does NOT do

The app does not use cookies, does not track you, does not collect analytics, contains no ads, and sends no data outside of Reddit — it has no access to external servers. It never sees your email, password, IP address, or any data beyond what Reddit exposes to apps.

Data deletion

If the original Reddit post is deleted, the app automatically and permanently deletes the corresponding board entry, including all vote records attached to it. Moderators can also remove any entry from the board. To have your vote removed, simply un-vote (tap the vote button again). For any other request, contact the moderators of the subreddit where the app is installed.

Third parties

No data is shared with third parties. All processing happens on Reddit's Developer Platform and is additionally subject to Reddit's Privacy Policy.
