About

feature-board is a Reddit app built on Reddit's Developer Platform (Devvit) by PinillosLab. It displays a community feature-request board inside a Reddit post. By interacting with the board you agree to these terms.

The service

  • Feature requests are created automatically from public subreddit posts that carry the "Feature Request" flair. The board itself does not accept direct submissions.
  • Votes cast on the board are limited to one per user per request and can be withdrawn at any time by the same user.
  • Board votes are informational only. They do not create any obligation to build, prioritize, or ship any feature.

Moderation

Subreddit moderators may change the status of any request (including marking it as declined) or remove it from the board at their sole discretion. Use of the underlying subreddit remains subject to Reddit's User Agreement and the subreddit's own rules.
