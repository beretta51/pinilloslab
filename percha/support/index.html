Percha Support

A quiet wardrobe OS — see what you own, what you wear, and what you don't. Currently in beta and actively evolving.

Need help?

Email nocion.exento_8u@icloud.com and I'll get back to you. Percha is made by an independent developer, Eduardo Pinillos — a real person reads every message. Beta feedback is especially welcome.

Getting started

  • Catalogue your wardrobe. Add the pieces you own so you can finally see everything in one place.
  • Track what you wear. Log wears to learn which pieces earn their spot and which never leave the closet.
  • Decide with data. Use what you learn to buy less, wear more, and let go of what you don't use.

iCloud sync

There are no accounts and no Percha servers. Your wardrobe data stays on your device and syncs across your devices privately through your own iCloud account whenever you're signed in to iCloud. There's nothing to configure.

Privacy

Percha has no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics. Read the full Percha Privacy Policy.
