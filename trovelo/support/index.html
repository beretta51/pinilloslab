Getting started

  • Create a trip. Give it a name and dates — Trovelo lays out one column per day so you can see the whole trip at a glance.
  • Fill the days. Add cards for sights, food, stays, and transport, with optional times, places, and prices. Ideas without a day yet live in the trip's Ideas bucket until you slot them in.
  • Track expenses. Add prices to your cards and keep an eye on your spending in dual currency — the trip's currency and your own.

iCloud sync

There are no accounts and no Trovelo servers. Your trips stay on your device and sync across your devices privately through your own iCloud account whenever you're signed in to iCloud. There's nothing to configure.

Privacy

Trovelo has no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics. Read the full Trovelo Privacy Policy.
